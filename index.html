Frequently Asked Questions

How old does my child need to be to use Mavri?

Mavri is designed for teens aged 13-18. Parents must create and link their account for teens under 18 years old.

Is my money safe with Mavri?

Yes! All Mavri accounts are FDIC-insured up to $250,000 through our banking partners. We also use bank-level encryption and security protocols to protect your information.

Can I use the card internationally?

Yes, the Mavri card works worldwide wherever Visa is accepted. There's a 1% foreign transaction fee for international purchases.

How do parents add money to their teen's account?

Parents can instantly transfer money from their linked bank account or set up recurring transfers for allowances. All transfers are free and happen immediately.

Can parents see all teen transactions?

Yes, parents have visibility into all transactions. You'll receive real-time notifications and can review spending in categorized reports.

What happens if my card is lost or stolen?

You can instantly lock your card in the app. Report it lost/stolen, and we'll issue a replacement card within 5-7 business days at no cost.
